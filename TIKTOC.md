# Cancer Research
## Tik TOC Research Spine

**Book type:** Course textbook
**Status:** Split from `cancer-biology-all`; research pass scaffold

## Core Thesis

Cancer Research teaches how cancer evidence is produced: screening tests, diagnostic tools, staging systems, treatment trials, and therapy modalities are all measurement and inference systems.
